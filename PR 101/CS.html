What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of the theory, design, development, and application of computer systems and computational processes.

Connnected field under Computer Science

Computer Scientist


A computer scientist designs and develops computer systems, algorithms, and models, applying mathematical and scientific principles to create innovative solutions and solve complex problems.


Expected salary: ₱79,400 per month (around ₱952,000 per year)

Computer Science Professional


A computer science professional is a broad term that encompasses various roles, including software development, data analysis, IT consulting, and more, applying computer science principles to drive business solutions and innovation.


Expected salary: ₱324,000 per year

Data Scientist


A data scientist extracts insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data using statistical and computational methods, helping organizations make informed decisions.


Expected salary: ₱803,059 per year
