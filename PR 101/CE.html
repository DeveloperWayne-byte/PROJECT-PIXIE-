What is Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the design, development, testing, and integration of computer hardware and software systems.

Connnected field under Computer Engineering

HARDWARE ENGINEERING


Hardware engineers design and develop devices such as computers, mobile phones, embedded systems, and networking equipment. Their work includes circuit design, PCB layout, firmware development, and hardware testing to improve efficiency, durability, and compatibility with software systems.


Expected salary: ₱36,167 per month

COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE & ORGANIZATIONS


Computer Architecture & Organization refers to the design and structure of a computer system, focusing on how its hardware and software components work together to execute tasks efficiently.


Expected salary: Software architect: ₱128,000 per month Network architect: ₱826,558 to ₱1,461,450 depending on experience.

NETWORKING & COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS


Networking & Communication Systems refer to the technologies and protocols that enable devices to connect, exchange data, and communicate efficiently.


Expected salary: ₱29,000 to ₱70,000

SOFTWARE ENGINEERING IN COMPUTER ENGINEERING


Software Engineering in Computer Engineering refers to the systematic approach to designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software within the field of computer engineering. It focuses on creating efficient, reliable, and scalable software solutions that interact with hardware components.


Expected salary: between ₱50,000 and ₱56,828
